Sour Cream Banana Bread

(631)
Recipe by Charishma_Ramchanda

656

SERVES: 12
YIELD: 1 loaf
UNITS: US

INGREDIENTS

  • 1
    cup sugar
  • 2

  • 1 1 ⁄ 2

DIRECTIONS

  • Grease 1 large loaf pan.
  • Cream margarine, sugar, eggs and vanilla.
  • Add dry ingredients, then bananas, nuts and sour cream.
  • Mix well.
  • Bake at 350 F for 1 hour.
